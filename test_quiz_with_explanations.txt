Question 1
In multimedia systems, what is a key difference in process management compared to traditional OS?
a) No scheduling required
b) Timing requirements must be considered
c) Only one process can run
d) Graphics processing only

ANSWERS
b) Timing requirements must be considered

DETAILED EXPLANATIONS
Q1: b) Timing requirements must be considered - Multimedia data handling imposes timing requirements that traditional OS scheduling doesn't consider. Real-time processing is crucial for audio and video streams to maintain quality and avoid interruptions.
